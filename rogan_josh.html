A flavorful curry featuring tender pieces of lamb or goat cooked in a rich gravy of yogurt, garlic, and spices. Originating from Kashmir, it's known for its deep red color and aromatic taste.

Rogan josh

Recipe:
1. Marinate Meat: Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices. Marinate meat for at least 1 hour.
2. Sauté Onions: Sauté onions until golden. Add marinated meat and cook until browned.
3. Add Spices: Add ground spices and cook for a few minutes.
4. Simmer: Add water, cover, and simmer on low heat until meat is tender.
5. Finish with Yogurt: Stir in yogurt and cook until gravy thickens.
6. Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or naan.
