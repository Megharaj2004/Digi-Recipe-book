A fragrant rice dish layered with aromatic spices, herbs, and either chicken, mutton, or vegetables. This one-pot dish is a staple of Indian cuisine, known for its complex flavors.

Biryani

Recipe:
1. Marinate Meat/Vegetables: Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, and marinate your choice of meat/vegetables for at least 1 hour.
2. Cook Rice: Parboil basmati rice with whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom).
3. Prepare Gravy: Saut√© onions until golden, add tomatoes, and cook until soft. Add marinated meat/vegetables and cook until done.
4. Layer Biryani: In a pot, layer half of the rice, followed by the gravy, and then the remaining rice. Sprinkle saffron milk, fried onions, and mint leaves.
5. Dum Cooking: Seal the pot with dough or a tight lid and cook on low heat for 20-30 minutes.
6. Serve: Fluff up the rice and serve hot with raita..
