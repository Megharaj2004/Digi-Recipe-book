A crispy, fermented crepe made from rice batter and black lentils, filled with a spiced potato mixture. It's a South Indian favorite often served with coconut chutney and sambar.

Masala dosa

Recipe:
1. Prepare Batter: Soak rice and urad dal separately for 4-6 hours. Grind to a smooth paste and ferment overnight.
2. Make Potato Filling: Saut√© onions, add boiled potatoes, turmeric, and salt. Mash and mix well.
3. Cook Dosa: Pour a ladle of batter onto a hot tawa, spread thinly, and cook until crispy.
4. Add Filling: Place the potato filling in the center and fold the dosa.
5. Serve: Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
