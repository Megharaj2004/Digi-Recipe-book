A rich, creamy dish featuring tender marinated chicken cooked in a spiced tomato-based curry sauce. It's a popular North Indian dish best enjoyed with naan or rice.

Butter chicken

Recipe:
1. Marinate Chicken: Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, and salt. Marinate chicken pieces for at least 1 hour.
2. Grill Chicken: Grill or pan-fry the marinated chicken until half-cooked.
3. Prepare Sauce: Saut√© onions in butter until golden, add ginger-garlic paste, and cook. Add tomato puree, spices, and cook until oil separates.
4. Simmer Chicken: Add the grilled chicken pieces to the sauce and simmer.
5. Finish with Cream: Stir in cream and fenugreek leaves, and cook for a few more minutes.
6. Serve: Garnish with coriander and serve with naan or rice.
