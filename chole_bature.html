A spicy chickpea curry paired with fluffy, deep-fried bread known as bhature. This Punjabi dish is a popular street food and a hearty meal.

Chole bhature

Recipe:
1. Cook Chickpeas: Soak chickpeas overnight, then boil with a pinch of baking soda until soft.
2. Make Masala: Saut√© onions, add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and spices. Cook until oil separates.
3. Simmer Chole: Add cooked chickpeas to the masala, add water, and simmer until thickened.
4. Prepare Dough: Knead flour with yogurt, salt, and baking powder to make a soft dough. Let it rest.
5. Fry Bhature: Roll out dough into circles and deep fry until golden and puffed.
6. Serve: Serve chole with hot bhature, and garnish with onions and lemon.
