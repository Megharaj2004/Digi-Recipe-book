Soft paneer cubes simmered in a creamy spinach sauce, infused with a blend of spices. It's a nutritious and flavorful dish enjoyed with rice or roti.

Palak paneer

Recipe:
1. Blanch Spinach: Blanch spinach leaves in boiling water for 2-3 minutes. Cool and blend into a puree.
2. Sauté Paneer: Lightly fry paneer cubes until golden.
3. Prepare Gravy: Sauté onions, add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and cook until soft. Add spinach puree and cook.
4. Simmer Paneer: Add paneer cubes, garam masala, and salt. Simmer for a few minutes.
5. Finish with Cream: Stir in cream and cook for a couple of minutes.
6. Serve: Serve hot with roti or rice.
