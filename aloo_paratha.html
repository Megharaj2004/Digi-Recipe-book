A stuffed flatbread made with whole wheat flour and filled with a seasoned potato mixture. Typically served with yogurt or pickle, it's a popular breakfast or brunch item.

Aloo paratha

Recipe:
1. Prepare Filling: Boil and mash 2-3 potatoes.
2. Add finely chopped onions, green chilies, coriander, cumin seeds, salt, and garam masala. Mix well.
3. Make Dough: Knead whole wheat flour with water and a pinch of salt to make a soft dough. Let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
4. Stuff Paratha: Take a small portion of dough, roll it out, place the potato filling in the center, and seal the edges.
5. Roll Paratha: Gently roll the stuffed dough into a flat circle.
6. Cook Paratha: Heat a tava (griddle), cook the paratha on both sides with some oil/ghee until golden brown.
7. Serve: Serve hot with yogurt, pickle, or butter.
